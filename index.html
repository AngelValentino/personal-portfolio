About me

Hi, my name is Angel, and I'm a self-taught software engineer who focuses on developing responsible and accessible user interfaces. I also enjoy sewing in my free time as a creative hobby.

I firmly believe that mastering the basics is essential for a deep understanding of any craft. Whether it's sewing or coding, the best way to learn is by diving in without training wheels. That's why I emphasize working with HTML/CSS and vanilla JavaScript, even though I'm proficient with libraries and frameworks such as React or Bootstrap.

Currently, I'm expanding my skills by learning back-end development with PHP and SQL.
